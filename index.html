Madrid is the capital and the largest city in Spain. This city has very warm weather in the fall but in the winter, around January through March, it gets very cold and you will require warm jackets and scarfs. Some days in the winter, it will be sunny and the cold weather is definitely bearable. In the spring and summer time, April through August is when Madrid has the most warmest weather.

Barcelona is a Mediterranean city with excellent beaches and good weather. Barcelona is sunny year round and it doesn't get too cold during the winter but you will need to dress warm some days! Barcelona is the most warm during the spring and summer.

The island of Mallorca is located off the coast of Spain east of Ibiza, and is part of the Balearic Islands. Compared to Ibiza, Mallorca is more of a calm city and does not have a big party scene. Mallorca is a fantastic year-round, holiday destination with lots of warm sunshine, and it does not get cold like Madrid and Barcelona.
